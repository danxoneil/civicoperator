Daniel X. O'Neil · Chicago

Creator across domains

A public-interest technologist who turns advanced data capability into systems people actually use — across private industry, public health, media, government, and civic organizations.

Throughout my career I've invented civic systems — and then built the organizations, methods, and tools to make them last. I co-founded EveryBlock, one of the first platforms to turn municipal data into news for your block. I founded Smart Chicago Collaborative and, inside it, created the Civic User Testing Group — paying everyday residents to test civic apps — and co-created Documenters, which pays community members to cover the public meetings that shape their cities.

The pattern repeats across private industry, public health, media, and government: find where data and public life meet, build the thing that connects them, and design it so the people it serves actually use it. Today that work runs through the Rural Health Transformation Grant Tracker and the consulting practice of Civic Operator.
